ABOUT US

Incepted in the year 1975, Amar Paints is an eminent manufacturer and supplier of paints and primers for various sectors like infrastructure, marine and industrial applications to name a few. With our dedicated efforts we are consistently providing quality paints to suit the specific requirements of our clients.

We believe in teamwork and encourage our workforce to put their best foot forward. Empowered by more than Four decades of experience, latest machinery and proficient workforce, we are providing international standard products, strictly adhering to client time frame.

In addition to this, we are committed towards maintaining the environmental standards and hence we offer paints that contain least hazardous raw materials.
